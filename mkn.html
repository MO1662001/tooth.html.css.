Dental fixtures



What is the dental installation process? To perform this operation, the doctor uses a tooth crown that is very similar to natural teeth, whether in appearance, color or function, so the patient does not feel any strangeness when seeing his teeth. Advantages of dental installation Many prefer to use various types of dental prostheses because of the multiple advantages they give, the most important of which are: They give an appearance that is very similar to natural teeth. Its success rate is high. Improves the process of chewing teeth. Reduce the risk of tooth decay. It preserves the jaw bones in place of the missing tooth. Reduces sensitivity in nearby teeth
